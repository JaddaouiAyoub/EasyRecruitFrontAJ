Get Ready for Your Interview!

Take a deep breath, stay confident, and get started when you're ready.

Tips for Success:

  • • Make sure you are in a quiet environment.
  • • Check your internet connection and camera/microphone setup.
  • • Keep your resume and any necessary documents nearby.
  • • Dress professionally, even for a virtual interview.
  • • Do not use a mobile phone for the interview.
  • • Attend the interview individually without any external help.
